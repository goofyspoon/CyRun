How To Play:
  • All Players: Use keypresses up, down, left, and right to move around the board.
  • PacMan: Collect resources to earn points. Grab a power pill to scare the ghosts and try to catch them to earn points.
  • Ghosts: Try to catch PacMan as many times as you can. Each time will earn you points.
  • When PacMan is caught, they will reappear at a random open spot on the board.
Play with Friends:
  • Either join an existing lobby or start a new one by entering the name below.
  • Share the lobby name to play against friends.
